Our Mission

Supporting and facilitating self awareness, self acceptance and skill building for young adults on the spectrum in higher education and the employment search.


Support young adults as they transition into the workforce. We seek to help individuals on the autism spectrum apply their social learning to the new setting of college, job interviews, and workforce interactions.


Our Vision

A world in which individuals on the spectrum are achieving their goals in higher education and employment and a world where employers appreciate and benefit from the talents of individuals on the spectrum.


Where people of all abilities are valued for their strengths and have their differences understood in the workplace to create greater innovation and success for all.


Our Core Values

At Spectrum Connections we interact with all individuals guided by the following core values:

  • Empowerment
  • Honesty
  • Respect
  • Understanding

  • Teamwork
  • Perseverance
  • Optimism
  • Resilience
